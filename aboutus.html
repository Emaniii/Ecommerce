About Us

135 Years in the Making

For 135 years Parker has continuously built upon a legacy of craftsmanship, innovation and quality; it’s this commitment that’s made the Parker name synonymous with excellence in Fine Writing.

In 1888, George S. Parker took the first of countless steps on a journey of ambition, passion, and innovation. He believed that it would always be possible to make a better pen, a belief that remains the mission and motivation behind every Parker craftsman today.

Over a century of innovation and hundreds of filed patents later, hand-assembly and rigorous hand-testing are still very much part of the Parker process. Our dedication and care for even the smallest details is our promise to deliver an exceptional writing experience.
